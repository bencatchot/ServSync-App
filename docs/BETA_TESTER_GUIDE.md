# ServSync Private Beta Tester Guide

Thanks for helping test ServSync. This beta is small on purpose. The goal is to test whether the everyday homeowner-contractor workflow feels clear and useful.

## What ServSync Is Testing

ServSync helps homeowners and contractors keep service work organized in one place:

- Homeowners can request work, review estimates and invoices, keep Home History records, and create manual reminders.
- Contractors can review requests, create estimates, create jobs, complete service/checklist work, send invoices, and keep customer work organized.

This is a beta. Some things will be improved based on your feedback.

## What Is Ready To Try

Please focus on:

- Service requests.
- Estimates.
- Accepted-estimate handoff to contractor work.
- Jobs and checklist/report workflows.
- Invoices.
- Filing eligible invoice/service records to Home History.
- Manual Home Reminders.
- Calendar visibility and event details.
- Support/feedback.

## Contractor Mission List

Try these if they match your real workflow:

1. Complete or review your business profile.
2. Add or review a homeowner/customer.
3. Review a homeowner service request.
4. Create and send an estimate.
5. Confirm the accepted-estimate next step is clear.
6. Create a job from an accepted estimate.
7. Complete service work or a checklist/report workflow.
8. Create and send an invoice where the customer is connected.
9. Review customer/home records and related work history.
10. Submit feedback if any step feels confusing, too slow, or missing context.

## Homeowner Mission List

Try these if they match your real workflow:

1. Add or review your home/property.
2. Connect with or use an existing connected contractor.
3. Create a service request.
4. Review an estimate.
5. Accept or decline an estimate when appropriate.
6. View an invoice.
7. File an eligible invoice to Home History.
8. Review Home History.
9. Create a manual Home Reminder.
10. Submit feedback if any step feels confusing, too slow, or missing context.

## Known Limitations

These are not live yet:

- Payments or Stripe checkout.
- QuickBooks/accounting sync.
- Push notifications.
- Email or text reminder automation.
- Recurring reminders.
- Native mobile apps.
- Full external calendar sync.
- Broad public Discover marketplace behavior.
- Public self-serve launch.

Reminder note: Home Reminders are manual in-app reminders. They do not send automatic emails, texts, or push alerts.

Invoice note: ServSync helps create and view invoices, but payment collection happens outside ServSync for now.

## How To Report Feedback

Best option:

- Use the in-app Support/feedback flow.

Early-beta backup:

- Send feedback directly to the founder if that is easier.

Please include:

- Are you using ServSync as a homeowner or contractor?
- What screen or tab were you on?
- What were you trying to do?
- What did you expect to happen?
- What actually happened?
- Did it block you from continuing?
- Screenshot if easy.

## What Counts As Urgent

Report these right away:

- You cannot log in.
- You cannot create or view a request, estimate, job, invoice, Home History entry, or reminder.
- You see another user's private information.
- You cannot complete a real workflow because the next step is unclear or broken.
- A mobile screen blocks a key action.

## What To Expect From The Beta

- The app is usable, but still improving.
- Some wording and workflows may change.
- Feedback is reviewed actively.
- Small issues may be fixed quickly.
- Bigger feature requests may be logged for later.

The most helpful feedback is specific: what you tried, where you got stuck, and what would have made the next step clearer.
