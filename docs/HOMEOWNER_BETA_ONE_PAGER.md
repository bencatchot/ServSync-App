# ServSync Homeowner Beta One-Pager

## Request Work Clearly And Keep Your Home History Organized

ServSync is a private beta app for homeowners and contractors who want a clearer way to keep home service work organized.

For homeowners, ServSync helps keep the service workflow in one place:

- Home/property details.
- Service requests.
- Estimates.
- Invoices.
- Home History.
- Manual Home Reminders.
- Calendar event details.
- Support and feedback.

This beta is small on purpose. It is meant for friendly homeowner testers who are paired with trusted beta contractors and willing to share honest feedback.

## What ServSync Is For Homeowners

ServSync helps homeowners request service, review estimates and invoices, keep useful home service records, and create manual follow-up reminders.

The current beta is focused on this practical homeowner path:

1. Add or review your home/property.
2. Create a service request for a connected contractor.
3. Review an estimate.
4. Accept or decline the estimate when appropriate.
5. View an invoice.
6. File eligible invoice/service records to Home History.
7. Create a manual Home Reminder for follow-up tasks.

The goal is not to make every home-service task automatic. The goal is to see whether ServSync makes the normal homeowner-contractor workflow easier to understand and easier to remember.

## Who This Beta Is For

Good fit:

- Homeowners who want a better place for estimates, invoices, Home History, and follow-up reminders.

- Viewing an invoice.

## What Is Not Live Yet

These are not live in the current beta:

- Payments or Stripe checkout.
- QuickBooks/accounting sync.
- Push notifications.
- Text or email reminder automation.
- Recurring reminders or scheduler automation.
- Native mobile apps.
- Full external calendar sync.
- Broad public contractor marketplace behavior.
- Public self-serve launch.

Invoices can be viewed in ServSync, but payment collection happens outside ServSync for now.

Home Reminders are manual in-app reminders. They do not send automatic emails, texts, or push alerts.

## What Feedback We Want

The best feedback is specific.

Please tell us:

- What you were trying to do.
- Which screen or step you were on.
- What you expected to happen.
- What actually happened.
- Where the workflow felt clear.
- Where you got stuck or hesitated.
- What felt too wordy, too hidden, or hard to use on your phone.
- Whether the app would help you keep home service work more organized if the rough edges were cleaned up.

- "I could create the request, but I was not sure what happened next."
- "I found the estimate, but the approval step needed clearer wording."
- "I liked filing the invoice to Home History, but I was not sure what was stored."
- "The reminder form worked, but I expected it to send a text."
