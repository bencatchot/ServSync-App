# ServSync Contractor Beta One-Pager

## Keep Service Work Organized From Request To Invoice

ServSync is a private beta app for small contractors who want a simpler way to keep service work organized.

It helps keep the everyday workflow in one place:

- Customer/homeowner requests.
- Estimates.
- Accepted-estimate next steps.
- Jobs and checklist/report work.
- Invoices.
- Customer/home work history.
- Calendar visibility.
- Support and feedback.

This beta is small on purpose. It is meant for trusted contractors who are willing to try the real workflow and give honest feedback.

## What ServSync Is

ServSync helps homeowners and contractors keep home service work organized.

For contractors, the current beta is focused on the practical job path:

1. Review a service request.
2. Create and send an estimate.
3. Turn an accepted estimate into a job.
4. Complete service work or a checklist/report job.
5. Create and send an invoice.
6. Keep the customer/home record easier to follow.

The goal is not to replace every tool overnight. The goal is to see whether ServSync makes the contractor workflow clearer, more professional, and easier to manage.

## Who This Beta Is For

Good fit:

- Solo contractors.
- Very small service teams.
- Contractors who already work with homeowners directly.
- Contractors who create estimates and invoices today.
- Contractors who are comfortable giving direct feedback during a private beta.

Best early workflows:

- Handyman and home repair.
- Lawn, pest, pool, cleaning, drywall, roofing/exterior, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, or similar local service work.
- Simple jobs where request, estimate, work, invoice, and follow-up matter more than heavy dispatch software.

Not a good fit yet:

- High-volume teams that need advanced dispatch, routing, technician roles, or admin reporting.
- Contractors who need Stripe, QuickBooks, automated reminders, native mobile apps, or full calendar sync before they can test.
- Contractors expecting immediate public marketplace lead flow.

During the private beta, contractors can test:

- Completing service jobs or checklist/report jobs.

```text
Request -> Estimate -> Accepted Estimate -> Job / Report -> Invoice
```

## What Is Not Live Yet

These are not live in the current beta:

- Payments or Stripe checkout.
- QuickBooks/accounting sync.
- Push notifications.
- Text or email reminder automation.
- Recurring reminders or scheduler automation.
- Native mobile apps.
- Full external calendar sync.
- Broad public marketplace lead generation.
- Public self-serve launch.
- Advanced analytics or admin reporting.
- Automated contractor call tracking.

Invoices can be created and viewed in ServSync, but payment collection happens outside ServSync for now.

Home Reminders are manual in-app reminders for homeowners. They do not send automatic emails, texts, or push alerts.

## What Feedback We Want

The best feedback is specific.

Please tell us:

- What you were trying to do.
- Which screen or step you were on.
- What you expected to happen.
- What actually happened.
- Where the workflow felt clear.
- Where you got stuck or hesitated.
- What felt too slow, too hidden, or too wordy.
- Whether the app would help with real customer work if the rough edges were cleaned up.

Helpful feedback examples:

- "I found the request, but I was not sure whether to create an estimate or schedule a visit."
- "The estimate flow made sense, but the job step needs clearer wording."
- "The invoice was easy to create, but I expected a payment button."
- "This screen worked on desktop but felt crowded on my phone."
